## The Story
Imagine paying someone to shoot dried peas at your bedroom window just to make sure you wake up on time! Before modern alarms, \"Knocker-Uppers\" were real professionals who roamed the streets with giant poles and pea shooters to ensure factory workers didn't oversleep.
