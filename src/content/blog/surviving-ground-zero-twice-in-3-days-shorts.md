## The Story
Tsutomu Yamaguchi is the only person officially recognized to have survived the atomic blasts in both Hiroshima and Nagasaki. After surviving the first explosion on a business trip, he returned home to Nagasaki just in time for the second bomb to strike while he was describing the first to his boss.
