## The Story
In the 1800s, the most expensive dentures were actually made from real teeth pulled off dead soldiers. After the Battle of Waterloo, looters harvested thousands of teeth to create \"Waterloo Teeth\" for wealthy aristocrats who refused to wear fake-looking animal bone.
