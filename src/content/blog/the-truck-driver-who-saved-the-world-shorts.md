## The Story
You won't believe how a frustrated truck driver completely revolutionized the modern world by inventing the shipping container. Malcom McLean's simple idea to skip port lines slashed shipping costs from $6 to just 16 cents a ton, making global trade possible. Subscribe for more amazing history facts!
