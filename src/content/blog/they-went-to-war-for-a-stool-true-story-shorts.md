## The Story
Did you know the British Empire once went to war just because they wanted to sit on a golden chair? Context: In 1900, the Ashanti Empire in Ghana hid their sacred Golden Stool to protect it from a British Governor who demanded to sit on it, sparking a massive rebellion led by Queen Mother Yaa Asantewaa.
